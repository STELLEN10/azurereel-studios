The Genesis

AzureReel was born from a desire to tell stories that bridge the gap between harsh reality and animated wonder. Founded in December after a series of minor structural changes, we emerged with a singular focus: 3D excellence.

We believe that anime is the perfect medium to explore the complexities of survival, education, and the strange cities we inhabit.
